# **App Name**: Knewrix Growth Partner

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Display: Render and display all website content, including homepage sections (Hero, Services Overview, Why Knewrix, Process, Testimonials), Service detail pages, About page narrative, and Footer, adapting for different screen sizes.
- Services Details Presentation: Provide dedicated sections for Digital Marketing, Marketing Strategy, Web & App Development, and Creative Editing, each detailing 'What we do,' 'Who it’s for,' and 'Outcome/benefits.'
- Portfolio & Case Studies: Display project cards on the Portfolio page, including client name, problem, solution, and results for each case study.
- About Page & Mission: Showcase the Knewrix story, mission to help brands grow smarter and faster, and position the agency as a strategic and execution partner.
- Contact & Lead Generation Form: Implement a simple contact form allowing users to submit inquiries directly, with backend integration for handling submissions (e.g., email notification, simple CRM entry). Also include explicit call-to-action buttons for 'Email' and 'Call booking CTA.'
- Strategic Call-to-Actions (CTAs): Strategically place high-converting CTAs like 'Start Your Growth,' 'Book a Strategy Call,' and 'Get a Proposal' across the site, linking to relevant contact or booking functionalities.
- AI-Powered Strategy Question Generator: A generative AI tool on the contact or services page where a visitor can input their business type and challenges, and the AI will generate a set of thought-provoking strategic questions relevant to their situation, encouraging deeper engagement and consultation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark. Embodying professionalism and a premium feel. The primary color will be a modern, confident blue (Hex: #3369D9) to represent strategy and technology. The background will be a very dark, slightly desaturated hue of the primary blue (Hex: #161C24), providing a clean canvas. A vibrant, analogous accent color (Hex: #4DD5F0) will be used for highlights and interactive elements, adding a dynamic, growth-focused pop.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, techy, and bold aesthetic, ideal for conveying confidence and impact. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for exceptional readability, clean lines, and neutral modernity, ensuring content clarity alongside the bold headlines.
- Minimal, clean line icons that align with a modern, high-tech agency feel, focusing on clarity and functionality over ornamentation.
- Emphasize clean whitespace and a structured grid system to highlight content, conveying professionalism and strategic organization. Key sections will utilize a hero, services overview, clear process steps, and prominent call-to-action banners.
- Subtle motion and hover effects on interactive elements like buttons, navigation items, and portfolio cards, providing a refined user experience without being distracting or flashy. Visual cues to indicate interaction states.
